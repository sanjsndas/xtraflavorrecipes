Cookie Policy

Like a well-balanced spice blend, cookies help us deliver a smooth, personalized experience on xtraflavorrecipes.com. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can control your preferences. By continuing to browse our earthy recipe haven, you agree to our use of cookies as described, except where you’ve configured your browser or our preference tool to reject them.

📖 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites recognize your device, remember your actions or preferences over time, and improve overall functionality. Cookies can be “session cookies” (deleted when you close your browser) or “persistent cookies” (remain until they expire or you delete them). We also use similar tracking technologies like localStorage and pixels, all covered under this policy.

🍪 2. How We Use Cookies

At Xtra Flavor Recipes, we use cookies to:

  • ✔ Ensure the website works properly (essential functionality, security, and load balancing).
  • ✔ Remember your preferences, such as language selection, dark/light mode, or saved recipe filters.
  • ✔ Understand how visitors explore our content — which recipes are most loved, and how we can improve navigation.
  • ✔ Deliver relevant recommendations (no behavioral ads from third parties without consent).
  • ✔ Analyze aggregated site traffic and performance using privacy-friendly analytics tools.

We do not use cookies to collect sensitive personal data, and we never sell cookie-derived information to advertisers.
