Privacy Policy

At Xtra Flavor Recipes (“we”, “us”, “our”), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website xtraflavorrecipes.com (the “Site”). By engaging with our earthy culinary community, you trust us with your information — and we take that seriously.

📌 1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you interact with the Site, subscribe to newsletters, submit recipe ideas, or contact our team. This may include:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number (optional) when you fill out contact forms or sign up for events.
  • Correspondence Data: Messages, recipe feedback, culinary questions, and any attachments you share.
  • Automated Information: Through cookies and similar technologies, we may collect usage data like IP address, browser type, referring pages, and site navigation patterns. This helps us enhance your flavor exploration journey.
  • Newsletter & Event Registration: If you opt-in, we store your email and preferences to send updates about brown-butter workshops, new recipes, and seasonal promotions.

🍪 2. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use first-party and third-party cookies to improve functionality, analyze traffic, and personalize content. Cookies help remember your preferences (like dark/light mode, language) and understand which recipes are most loved. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. For more details, please review our Cookies Policy. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they keep the site secure.

🔥 3. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us serve you better — like a slow-simmered stock. We use collected information to:

  • ✔ Respond to your inquiries, comments, or recipe requests.
  • ✔ Send occasional newsletters and marketing communications (only if you’ve given explicit consent).
  • ✔ Improve website content, user experience, and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • ✔ Host contests, polls, or culinary events and notify winners.
  • ✔ Detect, prevent, and address fraudulent or unauthorized activity.

We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your trust is our core ingredient.

🤝 4. Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information only in the following limited scenarios:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist with email delivery, analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), and form processing. They are bound by confidentiality and data protection agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation to protect our rights or the safety of our community.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred, with notice provided.

We do not share, trade, or rent your personal information for marketing purposes without your express approval.

🌰 5. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures — encryption, firewalls, and secure server environments — to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law (e.g., for tax/legal obligations). Contact form submissions are generally retained for 24 months to ensure we can address follow-up questions before anonymizing or deleting.

✍️ 6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR for EU residents, CCPA for California), you may have the following rights:

  • 🔹 Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • 🔹 Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • 🔹 Erasure (“Right to be forgotten”): Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal limitations.
  • 🔹 Restrict Processing: Ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • 🔹 Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • 🔹 Opt-out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from newsletters at any time using the “unsubscribe” link in emails.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Privacy Coordinator at privacy@xtraflavorrecipes.com or via mail (address below). We will respond within 30 days.

👶 7. Children’s Privacy

Our Site is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions). If we learn that we have inadvertently gathered data from a child without parental consent, we will delete it promptly. Parents/guardians may contact us if they believe their child has provided personal information.

📢 8. Third-Party Links

Our recipe pages or blog posts may include links to external websites (e.g., ingredient suppliers, kitchen tools). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

🔄 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The “Effective Date” at the top indicates the latest revision. Significant changes will be communicated via a notice on our homepage or email (if you have subscribed). Your continued use of the Site after modifications constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
